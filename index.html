What is MYNB

A utility token, built for real use

MYNB is a utility token that powers the My Neobank ecosystem. It follows the ERC-20 standard on the Ethereum blockchain, with a fixed supply and no mint function.

Its purpose is functional. MYNB rewards participation across My Neobank, a fintech offering crypto and multi-currency accounts for business and personal use, with an EU-focused launch centred on a Spain-Latin America payment corridor.

MYNB is issued by a Cayman Islands special-purpose vehicle, separate from the operating company.

What MYNB does

Three practical roles inside the My Neobank ecosystem. Nothing here depends on speculation.

Cashback rewards

Users earn MYNB as cashback on eligible activity across My Neobank. Value returned for using the accounts you already use.

Tier access & benefits

Holding or locking MYNB unlocks higher account tiers and enhanced benefits within the ecosystem.

Referral rewards

MYNB rewards referrals inside the ecosystem, aligning growth with the people who help it grow.
